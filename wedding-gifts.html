Wedding Gifts

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift of all!
If you feel inclined, in keeping with Vietnamese tradition,
we kindly invite you to consider a red envelope (lì xì) with a monetary gift.

lì xì symbolise luck, happiness, and blessings for the couple’s future.
Any amount will be appreciated and go towards fixing up our home!

We will have a box for the red envelopes on the day, or if you would prefer to, you can send the funds directly into our PayPal account: @BeckyShore58
