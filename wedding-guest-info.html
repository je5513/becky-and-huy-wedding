Accommodation

• Bourton Hall wedding venue is an exclusive wedding venue with bedrooms and is not a hotel. We do not have a reception area for checking in so please be patient when you arrive until one of our team members are available to show you to your room.

• Check in is from 12pm and no rooms for guests are available until after this time (our Chandelier Bar is open for guests arriving from 11am).

• Check in will be unavailable from 30mins before the wedding ceremony and until after 30 mins following the ceremony.

• All rooms are strictly non-smoking, and this includes vaping. There are outside areas available for smoking.

• There are no tea and coffee facilities in the bedrooms. There are complementary bottles of water. Tea and Coffee are available to order from the Chandelier Bar.

• Breakfast is served in the Dining Room from 8.30am and finishes at 9.30am

• Check out of rooms is by 10am and the building should be vacated by all guests by 10am. Late check out charges may apply.

• Please hand your room key in by the main doors when you leave or if you vacate your room before breakfast.
